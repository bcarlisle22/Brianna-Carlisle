02   ABOUT

Hello world!
I'm Brianna, a passionate and dedicated software developer with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology. My journey in the world of technology began in the hallowed halls of academia, where I was fortunate to gain a strong foundation in a wide array of fundamental concepts and programming languages. Throughout my academic career, I delved into the intricacies of Object-Oriented Programming, delved into the world of Relational Databases , honed my skills in UI/UX design , and became proficient in languages like Python , Java , C++, and SQL.

My appetite for learning and my enthusiasm for technology didn't stop at graduation. I embarked on a journey to explore the vast and dynamic realm of web development. In addition to my academic knowledge, I've immersed myself in the world of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript , crafting beautiful and functional user interfaces. I've embraced modern web development frameworks like Bootstrap , PHP , React , Vite.js , Next.js , and harnessed the power of Tailwind CSS to create stunning and responsive websites.

Beyond my professional life, I'm an avid reader, with James Clear's "Atomic Habits" standing as my all-time favorite. In my free time, I'm committed to broadening my horizons by teaching myself new languages, including Hindi and Spanish . My passion for learning extends to continually upgrading my skills and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies in the ever-evolving world of software development. Join me on my journey as I strive to make the digital world a more innovative and user-friendly place.
